About

My name is Aniesa and I'm a 17-year old black girl. Currently, I am a criminal justice pathway student studying to get into law so I know how pervasive police violence and excessive force. I remember all of the killings between the years 2014-2016 and I don't want a repeat of that. So, this website is here for anyone who wants to learn and figure out where to start in their journey to ending police brutality.
